Employers: Single touch payroll

This webinar provides small business employers with important information on how to effectively and efficiently report payroll tax for employees to the ATO.

Webinar for Australian businesses

  • Cost: $33.00
  • Location: Online
  • Date: Thursday 13
  • Time: 9:30 - 11:30 AM AEST

This FREE 1-hour session is for small businesses that are new to employing people and paying them for their work. As an employer, you are required to report salaries, wages, tax withheld and super information to the ATO through Single Touch Payroll each time you pay your employees.

Come and join this session to find out how it works, reporting options and how to report.
