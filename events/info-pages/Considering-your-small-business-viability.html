This webinar provides business owners with expert advice to help them determine the long-term viability of their business.

Are you at a business crossroads?

Unsure whether you should continue in business? Come along to this 45 minute session.

You'll learn how to use the ATO Business Viability Assessment Tool.

This tool provides information about your business viability to help you and your professional advisors in your decision-making. You will also receive the details of professional services offering free or low-cost advice to help inform your business decisions.
