Manual handling safety at work: Approaches to prevent injury (online session)

Last Updated: 13 July 2021

This webinar provides NSW employers with information about the PErforM (Participative Ergonomics for Manual Tasks) program to help them safely manage hazardous manual tasks in the workplace.

Details

Webinar

Free

SafeWork NSW is offering a free ONLINE session to help employers better manage hazardous manual tasks using the PErforM program.

The session will develop your skills so that you:

  • understand the PErforM program and participative ergonomics
  • understand the resources required to implement PErforM in your workplace
  • can deliver PErforM trainingÂ 
  • can facilitate a manual tasks risk assessment using the PErforM risk assessment tool.

This workshop is designed for people who:

  • will be implementing the PErforM program
  • occupy a position with access to management and workers
  • have good communication and motivational skills
  • have knowledge of risk management and hazardous manual tasks.

PLEASE NOTE: This webinar is specifically tailored for New South Wales businesses.
